Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for hiring a lawyer?

The process typically starts with an initial consultation where we discuss your case, understand your needs, and outline a legal strategy. After this, we enter into a formal engagement agreement, and then the legal work begins, with regular updates and communication.

Do you offer free consultations?

We offer an initial, brief discussion at no charge to determine if our services are a good fit for your case. A detailed, in-depth consultation may require a fee, which is discussed upfront.

Our firm specializes in a wide range of legal areas, including civil litigation, criminal defense, corporate law, constitutional matters, and family law. Our team has extensive experience across various courts in India.
